Property Crimes


  • Legal Grounds:
    • Burglary
    • Robbery
    • Larceny/Theft
    • Arson
    • Criminal vandalism
    • Trespassing
    • Burglary Tools Possession

  • Documentation required:
    • Incident Report
    • Photographs and Videos
    • Inventory of Stolen or Damaged Items
    • Witness Statements
    • Surveillance Footage
    • Police Report
    • Insurance Documents
    • Security System Records
    • Forensic Evidence
    • Financial Records

  • Time Commitment: ~ 3 years

    • Kidnapping
    • Sexual assault
    • Terrorist threats
    • Assault

  • Documentation required:
    • Incident Report
    • Photographs and Videos
    • Medical records

      • Forensic Evidence of Sexual Assault

Domestic Violence


  • Legal Grounds:
    • Physical abuse
    • Emotional or psychological abuse
    • Sexual abuse
    • Stalking
    • Economic abuse
    • Harassment
    • Violation of protective order
    • Child abuse or engagement
    • Distructio of Property
    • Interference with emergency services
    • Strangulation or suffocation

  • Documentation required:
    • Incident report
    • Photographs or videos

Hate Crimes


  • Legal Grounds:
    • Crimes committed on the basis of:
      • Race
      • Religion
      • Color
      • Nationality
      • Gender
      • Sexual Orientation
      • Gender Identity
      • Disability

  • Documentation required:
    • Incident report
    • Photographs or videos
    • Police report
    • Witness statements
    • Evidence of bias motivation
    • Hate symbols
    • Community records
    • Victim advocacy services records
    • Community response
    • Statements of impact

  • Time Commitment: ~ 2 months to 1 year
